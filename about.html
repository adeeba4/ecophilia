Why ecophilia?

As a group of three university students, we founded ecophilia with the goal to make sustainable living easier and more accessible for everyone. Since we are constantly surrounded by conversations about the future; we believe it is imperative we take into consideration the health of our planet. We understand that the topic of climate change, pollution, and carbon emissions can be overwhelming to think about without knowing what to do about it. We have also been in the position of feeling powerless to make a difference, yet after extensive research and volunteering all throughout Canada-we feel it is our duty to share our insights with the world.

We wanted to build more than just another website with facts about the environment-we yearned to create a movement! ecophilia is designed to help people make real, sustainable changes in their everyday lives, no matter where they're starting from. Whether you're looking for simple eco-friendly swaps, tools to track your environmental footprint, or resources to help you understand the impact you can make-we're here to make it simple and rewarding!

Together, we hope to inspire posibity change, one seed at a time. Thank you for being a part of the movement!
